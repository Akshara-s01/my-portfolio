A little about Me

Hi! I'm Akshara S., a passionate and driven Data Science Intern with a solid foundation in machine learning, deep learning, and data analysis. I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Computer Applications (BCA) at IGNOU. With a strong academic background and hands-on project experience, I have worked on various data science projects, including MBTI Personality Prediction and OLX Car Price Prediction.

Through my internships at Luminar Technolab and inGrade, I’ve developed skills in model development, data processing, and data-driven business solutions. I’m proficient in tools like Python, TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, and Power BI to build predictive models and create interactive dashboards that help organizations make informed decisions.

Beyond my work, I enjoy exploring new trends in data science, writing technical blogs, and contributing to open-source projects. My goal is to continue growing as a data scientist and make a lasting impact in the world of artificial intelligence and machine learning.
